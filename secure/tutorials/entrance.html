Playlists

Input all the required credentials. YOUTUBE LINKS MUST BE SEPERATED BY COMMA.

Note!Any links that do not fit youtube links will automatically be removed. Links that work will be added, but any other links will not be added.

You can always view your playlists by clicking on "View Playlists".

In order to play a playlist, follow these instructions.

  • Into the input box, type "playlist yourplaylistname" (I.E. A playlist named "bob" would be "playlist bob"
  • The playlist will automatically start playing. You don't have to click play.

As always, any regular youtube links will work.
